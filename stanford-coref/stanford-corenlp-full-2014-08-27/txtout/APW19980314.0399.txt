HEERENVEEN, Netherlands (AP) {{Gunda Niemann}} smashed the 3,000-meter speedskating world record Saturday in a time of 4 minutes 5.08 seconds, shattering the old record of {{Claudia Pechstein}} which stood at 4:07.13. {{Gunda Niemann}} began {{Claudia Pechstein}} race calmly and picked up pace in the middle laps before accelerating to the new record in the closing two laps at the indoor Thialf ice stadium. Racing in the next pair, {{Claudia Pechstein}} skated 4:07.36 for second place in the race, a time that left {{Claudia Pechstein}} second to Niemann in the all-round championship with just Sunday's 5,000-meter race left.
